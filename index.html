CORDOVA BLOG

GILUTUNGAN ISLAND

Gilutungan Island, nestled in the azure waters of Cordova, is a gem awaiting discovery. This pristine islet, surrounded by the cerulean Visayan Sea, embodies the epitome of natural beauty and tranquility.

CORDOVA PUBLIC COLLEGE

Cordova Public College stands as an invaluable asset to the community, shaping the minds of future leaders, innovators, and contributors to society. Its dedication to academic excellence, community involvement, and fostering holistic growth makes it a cornerstone of education within Cordova and beyond.

RORO PORT

RoRo ports feature ramps or platforms that allow vehicles and cargo to be rolled onto and off of vessels, streamlining the process compared to traditional cargo handling methods. This design enables quicker turnaround times for ships and efficient handling of diverse types of cargo.

BANTAYAN BAY

Bantayan Bay is a picturesque body of water located in the northern part of Cebu Province in the Philippines. Renowned for its stunning natural beauty and rich biodiversity, Bantayan Bay is a haven for both locals and tourists alike.

PAROLA SEA VIEW RESTAURANT

Parola Sea View Restaurant takes pride in its culinary offerings, boasting a menu that celebrates local flavors while embracing international influences. From fresh seafood caught daily to succulent grilled meats and vibrant vegetarian options, every dish is a harmonious blend of taste and artistry.

1OK ROSES

10,000 Roses has become an icon for visitors seeking the perfect backdrop for capturing timeless moments. The play of lights against the flowers offers photographers and visitors alike a canvas for creating unforgettable memories, making it a hotspot for picture-perfect moments shared with loved ones.
